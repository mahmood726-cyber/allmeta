Copas selection model — sensitivity

Sensitivity of the pooled effect under assumed selection mechanisms (Copas 2000). The selection parameter γ controls how strongly a study's precision (1/SE) determines publication. Output: adjusted estimate across γ.

Input

Sensitivity across γ

γ (selection strength)Implied unpublished fractionAdjusted pooled95% CI

Sensitivity curve

Interpretation: γ = 0 means no selection (observed = true). Larger γ attenuates effects more strongly. If the curve crosses zero as γ increases, the observed effect could be explained by selection; otherwise, it is robust.
Copas JB, Shi JQ. Meta-analysis, funnel plots and sensitivity analysis. Biostatistics 2000;1:247-262. Simplified implementation: per-study selection probability p(s) = Φ(γ_0 + γ_1 / s), back-weighted inverse-variance re-pooling. Full Copas MLE in metasens::copas.
