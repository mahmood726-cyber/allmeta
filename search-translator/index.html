Search Strategy Translator

Translate a PubMed search into Embase (Emtree) and Cochrane CENTRAL syntax. Field tags, MeSH, and operators are rewritten. This is a syntactic aid — always proof-read the output before running a real search.

PubMed source

Caveats:
  • MeSH → Emtree mapping uses a small built-in dictionary. Unmapped MeSH are emitted as free-text with a warning comment.
  • Embase /exp expands to narrower terms; PubMed MeSH major ≠ Embase major descriptors — review carefully.
  • Proximity operators (ADJn, NEAR) are not converted; use manual tuning.
