ROBINS-E — exposure studies

Risk Of Bias In Non-randomized Studies of Exposures (Higgins et al., 2022 draft). Seven domains covering confounding, exposure measurement, selection, missing data, misclassification, outcome measurement, and selective reporting.

Signaling questions

Judgement

Traffic light

Domains: D1 Confounding, D2 Measurement of the exposure, D3 Selection into the study, D4 Post-exposure interventions, D5 Missing data, D6 Measurement of the outcome, D7 Selection of the reported result. Overall: the worst domain (any critical → critical; any serious otherwise → serious; any moderate → moderate; else low). NI in any domain → overall NI.
