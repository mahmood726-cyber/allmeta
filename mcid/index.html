Use sensibly: MCID is context-dependent — it varies by baseline severity, direction of change, and the anchor used. Report more than one method and triangulate. For NI trials, the margin must be pre-specified and justified relative to the historical evidence base.
Distribution-based rules: Norman's 0.5·SD (Norman, Sloan & Wyrwich, Med Care 2003); 1·SEM (Wyrwich 1999); 1.96·SEM ≈ 95% smallest detectable change. Anchor-based: Juniper EF et al., J Clin Epidemiol 1994. NI margin: ICH E10, FDA guidance on non-inferiority clinical trials (2016).
