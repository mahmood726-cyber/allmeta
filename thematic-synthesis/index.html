Thematic Synthesis

Organise qualitative data for systematic reviews. Paste participant quotes, assign each to a descriptive code, then group codes under higher-order analytical themes. View the result as a hierarchical tree and export as JSON.

Hierarchical map

Method: Thomas & Harden (BMC Med Res Methodol 2008) three stages — line-by-line coding, development of descriptive themes, generation of analytical themes. This tool supports the second and third stages. The first stage (initial coding) is done by the reviewer.
