AMSTAR-2

A Measurement Tool to Assess systematic Reviews 2 (Shea et al., BMJ 2017). Rate each of 16 items as Yes / Partial Yes / No / NA. Seven items are critical domains; the count of critical flaws and non-critical weaknesses drives the overall confidence rating.

Items

Overall confidence

Critically low

Item-level

Critical domains (Shea 2017): 2 (protocol pre-registered), 4 (comprehensive search), 7 (list of excluded studies with reasons), 9 (risk of bias in included studies), 11 (appropriate meta-analytic methods), 13 (accounted for RoB when interpreting), 15 (investigated publication bias). Overall: High = 0–1 non-critical weakness; Moderate = >1 non-critical, 0 critical; Low = 1 critical; Critically Low = >1 critical.
